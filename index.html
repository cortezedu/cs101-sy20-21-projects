Your Voice is Power

MAKE BEATS. LEARN CODE. PROMOTE EQUITY.

In this 3rd quarter project, students learned about entrepreneurship, computer science, and social justice by remixing Pharrell's new song, Entrepreneur, on Georgia Tech's EarSketch platform. The lessons were part of Amazon, Georgia Tech, and Pharrell's philanthropic organization, YELLOW's coding competition.

Highlights

Paul C. is a top 10 winner for Round 1 and Katelyn V. is a top 100 winner for Round 1.

Command-Line Adventure Game

INTERACTIVE FICTION.

In this 4th quarter project, students learned about game design and computer science. Students had to create a command-line adventure game, also known as interactive fiction. Interactive fiction, often abbreviated IF, is software simulating environments in which players use text commands to control characters and influence the environment.
